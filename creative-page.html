This is my creative page! I decided to make just about my history with the Seahawks.

I have been a Seahawks fan my whole life but I remember first watching their games back in 2014, funnily enough this was the year that they won their first super bowl.

Since then, I have been keeping up with the Seahawks watching almost every single one of their games and hopefully they can win another super bowl, especially soon since in their recent history has been some of the best ever and they've made the playoffs almost every year in the last 10.
My favorite Seahawks player has always been Kam Chancellor, he played strong safety and was part of a historically good defence that the Seahawks had, it was nicknames "The Legion of Boom".

Football even inspired me to play some, I played tackle football from 6th to 8th grade, sadly I wasn't able to play in High School because of my parents' disapproval but it's not something I'm too bummed about now that I have been able to have a ton of other opportunities due to the opening in time of my schedule.
